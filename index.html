Nom Nominate Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 11, 2026

Nom Nominate ("we", "our", or "the app") is a group restaurant decision-making app. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Nom Nominate collects minimal data to provide its core functionality:

  • Approximate Location: We use your device's location to find nearby restaurants. This data is processed ephemerally (in real-time only) and is not stored on our servers after the session ends.
  • Display Name: A nickname you choose when joining a party. This is stored temporarily for the duration of the group session only.
  • Swipe Preferences: Your restaurant swipe choices during a session, used solely to calculate group matches. This data is deleted when the session ends.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information exclusively to:

  • Find restaurants near your location
  • Match your group's restaurant preferences
  • Display results to your party members

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your location data and preferences are only visible to members of your current party session.

Data Retention

All session data (party codes, swipe choices, group results) is temporary and automatically deleted when a session ends. We do not maintain long-term user profiles or data archives.

Data Security

All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. We use Firebase infrastructure, which provides industry-standard security measures.

Third-Party Services

Nom Nominate uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: For backend infrastructure and anonymous authentication
  • Google Places API: To retrieve restaurant information based on your location

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

Children's Privacy

Nom Nominate is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

User Accounts

Nom Nominate does not require user account creation. The app uses anonymous session-based authentication. No email addresses, passwords, or persistent user identifiers are collected.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store persistent personal data, there is no long-term data to access, modify, or delete. All session data is automatically removed when your session ends.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
